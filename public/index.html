How Morphe Works

Simple, powerful, and built for everyone

1. Download Morphe

Download the latest version of Morphe and install it on your your Android device.

2. Choose Your App

Select the app you want to modify. Currently Morphe supports YouTube and YouTube Music.

3. Apply Patches

Morphe will automatically patch your selected app.

4. Install & Enjoy

Install the patched app and enjoy your customized experience.
